Our History

Founded in 1985, Jalta Ruh Mission has been a beacon of hope and spiritual guidance for the community. Our journey began with a small group of faithful individuals gathering in a humble home, united by a shared vision of creating a welcoming space for worship and fellowship.

Over the decades, we've grown into a vibrant community that continues to uphold the values of compassion, service, and unwavering faith. Our mission remains steadfast: to illuminate lives through God's word and foster a supportive environment for spiritual growth.

Our Mission & Vision

At Jalta Ruh Mission, we strive to create a nurturing environment where individuals can deepen their relationship with God and with one another. We believe in the transformative power of faith and the strength that comes from community.

Our vision is to be a light in our community, extending God's love through outreach programs, spiritual education, and meaningful worship experiences that resonate with people from all walks of life.
